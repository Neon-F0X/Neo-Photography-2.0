Architecture and Street Photography:

Just a rather minimal horizontal picture of the details of the Pop Culture Museum at the Seattle Pacific Science Center taken during a gloomy day in Seattle, Washington

Not nesseccarily the best looking view, but as you can see, I am also a huge sucker for urban photography as well as for taking pictures of architecture that seem to have futuristic properties implmented to them. I am a massive fan of oppressive looking buildings that echo the corporate abyss most science-fiction media base their themes around, and the desperation of the atmoshpere these pictures contain.

Taken back in 2018, this is by far my most favorite cyberpunk-themed image I've taken in addition of a few edits of the lighting and colors to emit more of a futuristic look to the picture!
